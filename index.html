Who We Are

Capital With Conviction

Crom Capital was founded by three partners who believe the next great companies can be built anywhere, by anyone with the vision and drive to make it happen.

We are not passive check-writers. We are operators, strategists, and builders who take a 10-15% ownership stake because we intend to earn it. Through hands-on guidance, network access, and the operational muscle that turns ideas into institutions.

We invest in startups across technology, healthcare, consumer, and deep tech sectors. Our minimum investment is $250,000, with the ability to follow on in subsequent rounds.
